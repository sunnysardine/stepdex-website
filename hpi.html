Our Founder

The person who built it - and still runs it.

Sunny Law (Law Jiun Yuan) founded HPI Group with one conviction: that Malaysian SMEs deserve the same quality of leadership development that large corporations take for granted - delivered in a way that fits their culture, pace, and budget.

With 15+ years in HR strategy, leadership development, and adult learning, Sunny built the STEPDEX® framework from the ground up - drawing on industrial and organisational psychology, real SME experience, and a belief that great organisations are built one leader at a time. He co-authored STEP Up Your Performance with Dr. Henry Yeoh, presenting the work at the 44th ARTDO International Conference in Penang.

Our Philosophy

Four pillars. One enduring purpose.

Everything HPI does is guided by four foundational values - anchors that shape how we teach, how we coach, and how we lead.

真

Truth

Integrity in all things

Acting with honesty and authenticity - in our learning, our leadership, and our relationships with every client and learner.

善

Goodness

Leading with compassion

Helping others thrive - not just succeed. We build leaders who care about the people they lead, not just the results they produce.

美

Beauty

Excellence in craft

Creating workplaces that are not just productive, but truly excellent - where harmony, creativity, and human potential flourish together.

聖

Holiness

Purpose beyond profit

Striving for a higher purpose - building organisations and leaders who contribute meaningfully to their communities and society at large.
