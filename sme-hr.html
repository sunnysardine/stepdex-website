The Real Problem · 真正的問題

You're managing people without a system.

Most SME owners and managers weren't trained in HR. They were promoted, or they started the business. People management was learned on the job - through painful mistakes that could have been avoided with the right framework.

Hiring by gut feel

Interviews that feel like conversations but reveal nothing. Candidates who perform brilliantly under scrutiny then disappear once they start.

Job scopes nobody follows

Roles that exist on paper but don't match reality. KPIs that don't drive the right behaviours. Performance reviews that feel like box-ticking.

Managers who manage tasks, not people

Team leaders who were technical experts - now responsible for developing others but never taught how. Results: frustrated managers, demotivated teams.

Good people leaving for no reason

Staff who resigned and you still don't know why. Turnover that costs you 3–6 months of salary per person to replace, plus all the knowledge they took with them.

No development system

Training that happens reactively - when there's a problem. No clear career path. No system to grow people from within before you desperately need them.

Culture by accident

The team's culture is whatever it became - not what you intended. Values are on the wall but not in the daily decisions of your managers.

學
8-Week Curriculum · 八週課程大綱

Eight weeks. Every critical HR challenge solved.

One focused topic per week. Each session builds on the last - by Week 8 you have a complete, working people management system for your organisation.

01

Foundation

HR Fundamentals for SME Leaders

What HR actually means in an SME context. The people systems every growing business needs - and what happens when you build without them.

02

Hiring

Hiring Right the First Time

Assess candidates through their stories, not their rehearsed answers. Interview frameworks that reveal what a CV never can. Build a hiring scorecard that predicts performance.

03

Role Clarity

Job Scopes & KPIs That Drive Results

Write job descriptions your team can actually follow. Design KPIs that align individual work to business goals - not just metrics that look good on a report.

04

Onboarding

Making New Hires Succeed Faster

A structured first 90 days that reduces ramp-up time and increases retention. Most SMEs lose new staff in the first three months - this week fixes that.

05

Performance

Managing & Closing Performance Gaps

Diagnose whether a gap is a skill, will, or environment problem. Have performance conversations that lead to change - not defensiveness. Use the Performance Gap Analysis tool.

06

Development

Growing People from Within

Build a simple career pathing system your team believes in. Identify internal talent before they start looking elsewhere. Develop people proactively, not reactively.

07

Retention

Why Good People Stay (and Leave)

The real drivers of staff retention - and they're not salary. Build an environment where your best people choose to stay because they're growing, seen, and valued.

08

Systems

Building Your HR System

Pull everything together into a functioning HR system for your business. Leave with a complete playbook - not a folder of notes you'll never open again.
