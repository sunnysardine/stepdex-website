07Bilingual Rules
One system. Two dominant voices.
TC-primary for SME owners, family business, local Chinese market. EN-primary for corporate, MNC, regional, advisory contexts. Same soul. Different emotional entry.
Three Governing Rules
01
Whoever leads, leads fully
The dominant language gets hero scale, the gold word, and emotional weight. The secondary drops to ~35% — it explains, echoes, grounds. Never competes.
02
The layout breathes differently
TC characters are square and dense — layouts can be tighter. EN letterforms are horizontal — EN-primary needs more tracking, more air. The grid flexes. The soul doesn't.
03
Gold word holds in both
One gold word per layout regardless of language. TC: 不回避. EN: disappear. or test. The precision principle never changes. Only the language does.
