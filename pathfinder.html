Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

Why use Pathfinder instead of just ChatGPT? ▾

ChatGPT is a general-purpose tool. Pathfinder is purpose-built for career and leadership development - it's pre-configured with the STEPDEX® S.T.E.P. framework, focuses on professional growth, and asks structured questions to surface your real development gaps rather than giving generic advice.

Is my personal data safe with STEPDEX® Pathfinder? ▾

Yes. Pathfinder does not store, track, or share any personal data. No login is required. All inputs are session-based - once you close the chat, nothing is retained. It's designed to be a judgment-free, completely confidential space.

Is Pathfinder free to use? ▾

Access to the Pathfinder GPT is provided through our free bi-weekly Career Clarity Webinar. Register for the webinar to receive your access link - no payment required.

Does Pathfinder replace human coaching? ▾

No - and it's not designed to. Pathfinder is your between-sessions companion: ideal for reflection, preparation, and taking action in the moments when a human coach isn't available. For deeper transformation, our certified coaches and structured programmes are still the gold standard.

What language does Pathfinder support? ▾

Pathfinder works in English and Mandarin Chinese. Simply begin your conversation in your preferred language and it will respond accordingly.
